According to the U.S. Census Bureau QuickFacts, the population of Jacksonville city, Florida as of July 1, 2022 is **971,319** people¹. The city was incorporated on **February 9, 1832**¹. Jacksonville is located in the **Northeast region** of Florida¹. As for the classification of the city, it is considered **urban**¹. The average household income in Jacksonville is **$79,817** with a poverty rate of **14.87%** ². The median household income in Jacksonville is **$68,394** ¹. This is $5,332 higher than the median Florida household income and $1,323 less than the US median household income ¹.
