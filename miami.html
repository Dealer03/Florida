Miami is located in Miami-Dade County, Florida. According to the U.S. Census Bureau QuickFacts, Miami-Dade County had a population of **2,673,837** as of July 1, 2022¹. Miami was incorporated in **1896**². Miami is classified as an urban city². According to the U.S. Census Bureau QuickFacts, the median household income of Miami in 2021 was $57,8152. The average household income in Miami is **$79,886** with a poverty rate of **20.85%**⁴.

The median household income (in 2021 dollars) in Tallahassee for 2017-2021 was **$49,077**¹. According to Name Census, the average (or mean) household income in Tallahassee is **$73,485** with 5.74% of households classed as high income households (making $200,000+ per year)². According to City-Data.com, the median household income in Tallahassee, FL in 2021 was **$51,224**, which was 23.1% less than the median annual income of $63,062 across the entire state of Florida⁶.
